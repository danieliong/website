Graduate school presentation at UC Davis

November 8, 2019

In November 2019, I had the wonderful opportunity to return to my alma mater to speak about graduate programs in Statistics at UM and my personal journey to graduate school in Statistics. I also gave advice to prospective graduate students from my former department on how to make yourself competitive for graduate programs in Statistics (what courses to take, how to get involved with research, etc).
